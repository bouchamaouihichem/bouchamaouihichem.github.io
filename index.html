About Me

I am a PhD candidate in High Energy Physics at Princeton University. My research explores particle tracking in dense environments, convolutional neural networks in jet reconstruction, and exotic Higgs boson decays.

Beyond research, I enjoy teaching, mentoring, and helping build inclusive communities in STEM. Also: meme enthusiast, bodybuilder and fitness enthusiast, and a fan of Japanese animation.
