About Me

I am a PhD candidate in High Energy Physics at Princeton University. My research explores the use of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and other ML tools to improve particle tracking in CMS and enhance sensitivity to exotic Higgs boson decays.

Beyond research, I enjoy teaching, mentoring, and helping build inclusive communities in STEM. I'm also a meme maker, a bodybuilding and fitness enthusiast, and a fan of Japanese animation.
